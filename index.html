Game Rules:


  1. When two dice comes up as a 1 then the score for that round for the player is 0. Example: if the player rolls a 6 and 1, they get a score of 0

  2. If you roll a pair of the same numbers then the players score is the total of the two dice times 2. Example: if he player rolls 5 and 5, they get a score of (5+5)*2=20

  3. When you roll any other combination of dice other than the ones mentioned above then the score is the total value of the two dice. Example: player rolls a 3 and 2, player gets a score of 3+2=5
